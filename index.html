I design and deliver startup education programmes. I've designed and taught in some of the world's best (Seedcamp, UCL, Oxford), and most diverse (ClimateKIC, Creative Accelerator, Africa Prize For Engineering). Being a teacher is fun and challenging, but after years of this, I beleive developing peer-to-peer approaches will be a better way to keep up with fast-moving and localised knowledge needed in startups.

Since 2010, I've started Leancamp, Founder Centric, Village Accelerator and The Sources. These programmes span 4 continents, reaching tens of thousands of founders -- powered by 40 truly extraordinary people.

To take it ot the next level, we've recently united under a single organisation, Source Institite.

I live in Sofia, Bulgaria, but my work often takes me to London, where I lived for 10 years, and around Africa.

You can contact me at smile@saintsal.com and stay in touch by getting my posts and essays by email.

If you're wondering, the "Saint" in SaintSal is from a film where the protagonist confronts his life's ambitions in a weekend. It reminds me that life's short and to make the most of every day!
