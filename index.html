I design and deliver startup education programmes, focusing on practical education and peer-to-peer approaches that keep up with fast-moving and localised knowledge needed to succeed in tech startups. I started Leancamp, Founder Centric, Village Accelerator and The Sources. All told, these programmes span 4 continents, reaching tens of thousands of founders -- powered by 40 truly extraordinary people. To take it ot the next level, we've recently united under a single organisation, Source Institite.

I live in Sofia, Bulgaria, but my work often takes me to London, where I lived for 10 years, and around Africa.

You can contact me at smile@saintsal.com and stay in touch by getting my posts and essays by email. If you're wondering about the "Saint" in the SaintSal moniker, it's a reference to Things To Do In Denver When You're Dead, a film where the protagonist - Jimmy The Saint - is forced to confront his life's ambitions.
